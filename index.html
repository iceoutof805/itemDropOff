ITEM DROP OFF INFORMATION

Santa Barbara Donation Site + Information

Casa de la Raza at 601 E Montecito St in downtown Santa Barbara is currently accepting donations of perishable and nonperishable food, hygiene supplies, home supplies, baby and kids supplies, and other items. A full list can be found on their instagram @lacasadelaraza.sb. Call to arrange a drop-off at (805)695-1229.

Oxnard Donation Site + Information

The Mystery Shop at 420 A St in Oxnard is currently accepting food and basic amenity donations for families impacted by the raids in Camarillo and Carpenteria. They are open from 12:30PM - 10PM daily.
